First Aid

First Aid is the immediate, temporary medical assistance given to a person who is ill or injured before professional help arrives. Having this skill is very beneficial in the workplace as accidents can happen at any time. This courses teaches you all you need to know about first aid, especially on how to deal with specific injuries.
