Child Minding

Child Minding is the care and supervision of children, typically in a home setting. This course teaches you all you need to know about child minding, from basic childcare to more advanced techniques.

Course Content

-Introduction to Child Minding

-Child Development

-Health and Safety

-Nutrition and Meal Planning

-Activities and Play

-Behavior Management

-Communication with Parents

-Emergency Procedures
