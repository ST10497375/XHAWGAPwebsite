Sewing

Sewing is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a needle and thread. This course teaches you all you need to know about sewing, from basic stitching techniques to more advanced projects.

Course Content

-Introduction to Sewing

-Basic Sewing Techniques

-Hand Sewing

-Machine Sewing

-Fabric Types and Selection

-Pattern Reading and Cutting

-Alterations and Repairs
