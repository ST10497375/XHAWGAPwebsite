Garden Maintenance

Garden Maintenance involves the upkeep and care of gardens and outdoor spaces to ensure they remain healthy, attractive, and functional. This course teaches you all you need to know about garden maintenance, from basic gardening skills to more advanced techniques.

Course Content

-Introduction to Garden Maintenance

-Soil Preparation and Fertilization

-Planting and Transplanting

-Watering and Irrigation

-Weeding and Pest Control

-Pruning and Trimming

-Seasonal Garden Tasks
