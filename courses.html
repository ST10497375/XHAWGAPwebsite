Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses, with all of our professors skilled and ready to teach.



You can choose between:

Long-term courses (6 months)


-First Aid
First Aid is the immediate, temporary medical assistance given to a person who is ill or injured before professional help arrives. Having this skill is very beneficial in the workplace as accidents can happen at any time. This courses teaches you all you need to know about first aid, especially on how to deal with specific injuries.

-Sewing
Sewing is the craft of fastening or attaching objects using stitches made with a needle and thread. This course teaches you all you need to know about sewing, from the basics to more advanced techniques.

-Landscaping
Landscaping involves modifying the visible features of an area of land, including living elements such as flora and fauna, as well as natural elements such as landforms, terrain shape and elevation, and bodies of water. This course teaches you all you need to know about landscaping, from the basics to more advanced techniques.
-Life Skills
Life skills are abilities that help individuals effectively handle the demands and challenges of everyday life. This course teaches you all you need to know about life skills, from the basics to more advanced techniques.

Short-term courses (6 weeks)



-Child Minding
Child minding is the care and supervision of a child or children, usually in the child's own home, by a person other than the child's legal guardian. This course teaches you all you need to know about child minding, from the basics to more advanced techniques.

-Garden Maintenence
Garden maintenance involves the upkeep and care of a garden, including tasks such as planting, watering, weeding, pruning, and fertilizing. This course teaches you all you need to know about garden maintenance, from the basics to more advanced techniques.
-Cooking
Cooking is the art, science, and craft of preparing food for consumption. This course teaches you all you need to know about cooking, from basic, quick dishes to more advanced meals.
